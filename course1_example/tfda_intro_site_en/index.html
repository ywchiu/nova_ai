Agency Mission

The Taiwan Food and Drug Administration (TFDA) is responsible for the safety management of food, drugs, medical devices, and cosmetics, protecting public health. We establish comprehensive regulatory frameworks based on science and centered on public health.

🛡️

Safety Oversight

Establishing rigorous safety management standards to ensure product quality

🔬

Science-Based

Formulating policies and regulations based on scientific evidence

🤝

International Integration

Participating in international organizations and sharing safety management experience

👥

Public-Focused

Public health and rights as our guiding principle

Four Business Areas

Comprehensive safety management for Food, Drugs, Medical Devices, and Cosmetics

Food Regulatory Management

Establishing food-related regulations and standards to ensure food safety and protect consumer rights.

  • Inspection standards for food and food products
  • Management of food additive usage regulations
  • Review of food ingredient and nutrition labeling

Inspection and Monitoring

Implementing food market sampling and risk monitoring to ensure compliance with safety standards.

  • Regular food market sampling programs
  • Imported food inspection and quarantine
  • Handling of food violation cases

Labeling and Claims

Reviewing food labels and advertising claims to prevent false or exaggerated promotions.

  • Review of food nutrition and efficacy labels
  • Legality assessment of advertising claims
  • Consumer information provision

Drug Review

Executing drug safety, efficacy, and quality reviews to ensure safe and effective medication.

  • New drug application technical review
  • Drug manufacturing license review
  • Clinical trial plan approval

Pharmacovigilance

Implementing adverse drug reaction monitoring and risk management measures.

  • Adverse drug reaction reporting system
  • Drug safety risk assessment
  • Implementation of control measures when necessary

Traditional Medicine

Establishing management standards for traditional medicine products.

  • Traditional medicine application review
  • Traditional medicine product regulations
  • Scientific evidence collection and assessment

Device Review

Executing medical device safety and efficacy review and management.

  • Medical device application review
  • Technical document review and assessment
  • Clinical trial review

Controlled Devices

Implementing stricter management measures for high-risk controlled devices.

  • Controlled device license review
  • Post-market tracking of implantable devices
  • Adverse event handling

IVD Management

Managing the quality and safety of in vitro diagnostic medical devices.

  • IVD device review
  • Quality system inspection
  • Market surveillance

Cosmetic Review

Executing safety assessment and review management for cosmetics before market release.

  • Pre-market cosmetic registration
  • Ingredient safety assessment
  • Label and claim review

Market Surveillance

Implementing cosmetic market sampling and handling violation cases.

  • Regular market product sampling
  • Handling of violation cases
  • Consumer complaint processing

Ingredient Management

Managing allowable and prohibited ingredients in cosmetics.

  • Updates to allowable ingredient lists
  • Assessment of new technology ingredients
  • International regulation tracking
